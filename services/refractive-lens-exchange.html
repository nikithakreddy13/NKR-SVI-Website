Vision Correction Surgery

Replace Your Lens,
Transform Your Vision.

Refractive Lens Exchange (RLE) — also called Clear Lens Exchange — replaces your eye’s natural lens with a premium IOL to correct nearsightedness, farsightedness, or presbyopia. It also permanently eliminates the possibility of future cataracts.

Am I a Candidate?

The Procedure

What Is Refractive Lens Exchange?

RLE uses the same microsurgical technique as modern cataract surgery — phacoemulsification with IOL implantation. The key difference is that in RLE, the natural lens being removed is clear, not yet clouded by cataracts. By replacing the lens proactively with a premium IOL, patients can correct significant refractive errors while simultaneously preventing cataracts from ever developing. RLE is particularly valuable for patients in their 40s, 50s, and 60s who are experiencing presbyopia (age-related loss of near vision) or who have prescriptions too strong for LASIK. It offers a permanent, one-time solution that eliminates both glasses dependency and future cataract surgery.

Permanent Vision Correction

Unlike glasses or contacts that treat symptoms, RLE corrects the root cause by replacing the lens itself. Results are permanent.

Eliminates Future Cataracts

Because RLE replaces your natural lens entirely, cataracts can never develop in that eye. One procedure, two problems solved.

Multiple Premium IOL Options

Choose from multifocal, EDOF, toric, or Light Adjustable Lens options to match your lifestyle and visual goals.

Safe for Non-LASIK Candidates

RLE is an excellent alternative for patients with thin corneas, high prescriptions, or dry eye who aren’t suited for laser vision correction.

Ideal Candidates

Is RLE Right for You?

Ages 40–65 with Presbyopia

If reading glasses have become a constant companion and you want permanent freedom from them, RLE delivers.

High Prescriptions Beyond LASIK Range

Prescriptions stronger than −8 or +6 diopters can be fully corrected with RLE when LASIK isn’t an option.

Patients Who Want to Prevent Cataracts

If cataracts run in your family or you want to avoid future surgery, RLE eliminates that possibility entirely.

What to Expect

Your RLE Journey Step by Step

01

Comprehensive Refractive Evaluation

A thorough eye exam including corneal mapping, biometry, and a detailed review of your prescription history and visual goals. We determine candidacy and discuss IOL options.

02

IOL Selection

Together, we select the premium IOL that best matches your lifestyle — multifocal for full-range glasses freedom, EDOF for smooth distance-to-intermediate, toric for astigmatism correction, or LAL for maximum precision.

03

Same-Day Lens Exchange

The outpatient procedure takes under 20 minutes per eye. You’re awake but comfortable with topical anesthesia. No needles, no stitches. A driver takes you home to rest.

04

Rapid Visual Recovery

Most patients notice improved vision within 24–48 hours. Full stabilization typically occurs within 4–6 weeks. Your surgeon sees you at every follow-up visit.
