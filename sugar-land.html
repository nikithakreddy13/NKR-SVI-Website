Directions

Getting Here from Sugar Land

From Sugar Land, take US-59/I-69 North to the Grand Parkway (SH-99) West, then follow SH-99 to US-290 West. Exit at Jarvis Road — our office at 27700 Northwest Freeway, Suite 390 is right off the highway. The drive from Sugar Land Town Square takes approximately 35 to 40 minutes.

Patients from First Colony, New Territory, Sweetwater, and Riverstone may also take Westpark Tollway to the Grand Parkway North as an alternative route. Free parking is available at the building entrance.
