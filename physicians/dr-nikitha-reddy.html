Biography

Fellowship-Trained. Patient-Focused.

Dr. Nikitha Reddy is a board-certified, fellowship-trained ophthalmologist who brings world-class expertise and genuine compassion to every patient she treats at Soni Vision Institute. Her advanced training from two of the country’s most prestigious programs gives her a rare combination of surgical precision and clinical depth.

Dr. Reddy earned her medical degree and completed her ophthalmology residency at UT Southwestern Medical Center in Dallas — consistently ranked among the top ophthalmology programs in the nation. She then pursued a highly competitive Glaucoma, Anterior Segment, and Advanced Surgery (GAASS) fellowship at the University of Toronto under the mentorship of Dr. Ike Ahmed, one of the most renowned glaucoma and anterior segment surgeons in the world.

This combination of elite residency and fellowship training allows Dr. Reddy to offer her patients a uniquely comprehensive approach. Whether performing complex cataract surgery with advanced technology lenses, treating glaucoma with the latest minimally invasive techniques, or helping patients achieve visual freedom through refractive procedures, she brings the same meticulous attention to detail and personal care to every case.

Dr. Reddy has been practicing in Houston for several years and has quickly become one of the most trusted eye surgeons in the greater Houston area. Her patients consistently describe her as warm, thorough, and exceptionally skilled — someone who makes them feel at ease while delivering outstanding surgical results.
